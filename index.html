Types Of Coffee Beans



Arabica This is the most popular type of coffee bean,
accounting for nearly 60% of all coffee beans in the world.
Arabica beans are known for their mild, sweet flavor and low acidity.
Price per kilo is:4.046 USD/kg

         

Robusta beans are the second most popular type of coffee bean,
making up 40% of all worldwide bean exports. They are known for their strong,
bitter flavor and high caffeine content.

Best Beverages Of Coffee



Turkish Coffee

Turkish coffee is very finely ground
coffee brewed by boiling.Any coffee bean
may be used; arabica varieties are considered
best,but robusta or a blend is also used.

         

Espresso

Espresso is a concentrated form of coffee
served in small, strong shots and is the base
for many coffee drinks. It's made from the same
beans as coffee but is stronger, thicker, and higher
in caffeine. However, because espresso is typically
served in smaller servings than coffee,
it has less caffeine per serving.

         

Cappuccino

Cappuccino is a coffee drink made from
a mixture of espresso and steamed milk.
A traditional Italian cappuccino is generally
a single (or sometimes a double) espresso shot
topped with equal parts steamed and frothed
milk in a ratio of 1:1:1. Nowadays, a cappuccino
is an espresso-based hot coffee beverage,
The typical cappuccino recipe consists of 1 1/3
double espresso shot, 2 1/3 part steamed milk,
and 3 1/3 part milk foam.
